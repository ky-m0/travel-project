Discover Salem, where history meets mystery

Welcome to Salem, Massachusetts, a city steeped in history, mystery, and a touch of magic. Known as the "Witch City," Salem is renowned for its captivating blend of colonial heritage and intriguing occult lore. Wander through Salem's charming streets, visit the Witch Museum and savour fresh New England cuisine at its finest.

Browse mystical boutiques, uncover haunted histories on candlelit tours, and discover a town where every corner whispers secrets from the past. From potions and tarot to ghost stories and cobblestone charm, Salem is a place where the veil between worlds feels just a little thinner.

Things to see and do in Salem

Salem Witch Museum

The Salem Witch Museum offers an immersive journey into the infamous 1692 witch trials, bringing history to life through dramatic storytelling and haunting exhibits.

Address

19 1/2 N Washington Square, Salem

Best time to visit

Open year-round. Presentations run every half-hour from 10:00 am to 4:30 pm, with extended hours during July, August, and October.

Essex Street

Essex Street’s lively pedestrian mall buzzes with energy—colorful shops, street performers, and cozy cafés create a vibrant yet charming heart where Salem's magic comes alive.

Address

Essex Street, Salem

Best time to visit

In October, the entire area transforms into a Halloween lover's dream.

The House of the Seven Gables

Shrouded in mystery and legend, the House of the Seven Gables is said to harbor restless spirits and centuries-old secrets behind its weathered walls.

Address

115 Derby Street, Salem

Best time to visit

Late September through October. Try weekday mornings or early afternoons to avoid crowds.
